The Vision Behind ROBETS TRAVEL AGENTS

Robet K. Mugisha is a passionate entrepreneur and travel enthusiast who founded ROBETS TRAVEL AGENTS with a simple mission: to make travel in Uganda safe, reliable, and enjoyable for everyone.

Born and raised in Kampala, Robet grew up with a deep love for Uganda's diverse landscapes and rich culture. He noticed that many visitors struggled with transportation, accommodation, and navigating the city. This inspired him to create a travel agency that would serve as a trusted partner for both locals and foreigners.

Today, ROBETS TRAVEL AGENTS is a 24/7 service that specializes in airport pickups, hotel reservations, car hire, and guided tours across Kampala and beyond. The company has served hundreds of happy travelers from around the world.

The Journey

Robet started his journey in the travel industry in 2012, working as a tour guide for various safari companies. He quickly realized that there was a gap in the market for reliable, personalized travel services in Kampala.

With his savings and a single vehicle, he launched ROBETS TRAVEL AGENTS from a small office in Kampala. His dedication to customer service and attention to detail helped the business grow rapidly.
